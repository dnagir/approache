I am a one of those developers who uses Borland Developer Studio 2006. Now I just lost my patience about it. I have a class with internal method accessors. I need to publish the interface of this class (eg: make it public). I don't know better way in BDS to do it except using Replace tool. So I use this stupid way. I type into Text to find "internal " and Replace with "public ". I check "Case sensitive" and "Prompt on replace" boxes and use Global scope (entire file). I click "Replace All" button... It does prompts me to replace some entries. But after second or third one IDE hangs with 100% CPU. So I kill it. Tried to do it many times, but my time was just wasted. I only have to wait while the BDS-crocodile starts and then eats my CPU with no response. I'll do it by hands... What a shame! I always thoutgh nowadays IDEs are time savers. I'm wrong. Its majesty BDS's animal reminds me about having a free IDE (SharpDevelop)...
