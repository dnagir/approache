Just a list of resources that might be useful for a lot of people:
  • .NET Rocks - a weekly talk show for anyone interested in programming on the Microsoft .NET platform.
  • Hanselminutes - a weekly audio talk show with noted web developer and technologist Scott Hanselman and hosted by Carl Franklin. Scott discusses utilities and tools, gives practical how-to advice, and discusses ASP.NET or Windows issues and workarounds.
  • Alt.NET podcast – number of podcasts on a lot of topics around .NET (BTW, here’s Aussie Alt.NET site).
  • Herding Code - a weekly podcast with K. Scott Allen, Kevin Dente, Scott Koon, and Jon Galloway.
  • ASP.Net Podcast - brings a technology focus to the area of podcasts.  This podcast is geared towards developers with applications that scale to a large amount of data and users.
  • Deep Fried Bytes - an audio talk show with a Southern flavor hosted by technologists and developers Keith Elder and Chris Woodruff. The show discusses a wide range of topics including application development, operating systems and technology in general. Anything is fair game if it plugs into the wall or takes a battery.
  • Elegant Code - a blogging community of passionate software developers, architects, and technologists who share a feed.
  • Polymorphic Podcast – podcasts by Craig Shoemaker, a software developer, podcaster, blogger and New-Media Evangelist for Infragistics. As host of the Polymorphic Podcast and Pixel8, Craig does what he loves most - making contributions to the community and drawing the best out of industry luminaries.
  • Sparkling Client – The Silverlight podcasts.
  • Sharepoint Pod Show -  A Podcast All About SharePoint.
  • DimeCasts.NET – inform and educate in ~10 minutes or less.
  • Summer of NHibernate - one-stop easy access to direct downloads of the screencasts related to NHibernate.
Hope you’ll find a lot of useful stuff on those sites.
